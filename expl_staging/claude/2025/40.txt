정답: ④ (4번)

해설:
ITIL 4 Service Value System(SVS)의 Practice 분류를 묻는 문항이다. ITIL 4 SVS는 3가지 Practice 범주로 구성된다.

ITIL 4 Practice 3가지 범주:
1. General Management Practices (14개): 전사적 관리 실천
2. Service Management Practices (17개): IT 서비스 관리 특화
3. Technical Management Practices (3개): 기술 관련 실천

Service Management Practices (17개) 주요 목록:
Availability Management, Business Analysis, Capacity & Performance Management, Change Control, Incident Management, IT Asset Management, Monitoring & Event Management, Problem Management, Release Management, Service Catalogue Management, Service Configuration Management, Service Continuity Management, Service Design, Service Desk, Service Level Management, Service Request Management, Service Validation & Testing.

각 보기 분석:
① Business Analysis — Service Management Practices에 포함. 적절.
② Change Control — Service Management Practices에 포함. 적절.
③ IT Asset Management — Service Management Practices에 포함. 적절.
④ Software Deployment and Management — Technical Management Practices의 하나인 'Deployment Management'(소프트웨어 배포 및 관리)에 해당한다. Service Management Practices가 아닌 Technical Management Practices이므로 부적절. 정답.

ITIL 4 Technical Management Practices 3개:
1. Deployment Management (= Software Deployment and Management)
2. Infrastructure and Platform Management
3. Software Development and Management

핵심 정리:
Software Deployment and Management = Technical Management Practice (3개 중 하나).
Service Management Practices(17개)와 Technical Management Practices(3개) 구분이 핵심.
IT Asset Management, Change Control, Business Analysis는 모두 Service Management Practices.
